It appears that I shall be some time wandering AnraithI blame my companions, fully and completely. before returning to my treasured Tower. In the despair induced by this anticipated long absence, I see a spark of hope: nowhere in the Great Library's vast collection is a treatise that summarizes all the known world in a single voice during a single era. Could this be my contribution to that venerable institution? As circumstance propels me to the edges of the world, I shall collect my thoughts and observations even as I labor toward my ultimate goal: the methods of magical creation. Thus my quill shall remain inked, my eye clear, and my wits sharp!

Here find you the beginnings of my modest work, The Encyclopædia Anraith!
