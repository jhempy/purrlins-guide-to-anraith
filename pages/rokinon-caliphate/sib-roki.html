The 5 sets of paired deities that make up the caliphates pantheon. The Sib-Roki consist of the following gods and goddesses The god of diplomacy and the goddess of war The god of death/harvest and the goddess of fertility/planting The god of the sea and the goddess of the sky The god of knowledge/writing and the goddess of trade/trickery The god of Music/art and the goddess of Forge/craft
