--- layout: page title: The Sabva Republic ---

The Sabva Republic is a collection of autonomous, culturally unique city-states under loose control of a centralized Council of Envoys.

Climate: Temperate

Terrain: Mountains cut through the center of the Republic. Rocky archipelago to the northeast. Forests in the northwest. Fjords along the southern and eastern coasts, separated by pockets of farmable land.

Exports: Fine craft work, silver ore

Capital City: Alkabittle

Prominent Religion(s): Varies by city-state

Political Structure: Each city-state is governed by a Primus

Language(s): Common, Sabvian, distinct dialects within each city-state

Major Cities:

  • Adrak
  • Alkibittle
  • Fannhia
  • Ja-A'l
  • Kinzwia
  • Phauja
  • Sari-El
  • Urdak

Other Points of Note: Sabvians tend to be welcoming of travelers, as many city-states were settled by migrants and the quest for a home still resonates with them.
