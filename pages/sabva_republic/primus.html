The Top authority in a Sabva city state. Every city has a different process for electing their Primus. For example in the city of Phauja the current primus can be challenged at any point and usurped with a hand to hand fight to the death, whereas in Sari-El the council of elders picks a goldfish with a name on it from a pond every three years. Aside from running the city the Primus also appoints that cities representative to the to the council of envoys.
