Prior to about 1250 CEThis designation properly stands for "Common Era," but the Rokinon insist it stands for "Emergence of Caliph"., each of the nations of Anraith maintained a separate calendar. Increased cooperation between the nations revealed a need for universal timekeeping, and the calendar in use today was created. While no one nation has complete control of the calendar's final form, names were chosen for the months based on the cultural, political, and ethnic variety throughout Anraith, and thus all are represented to some degree.
